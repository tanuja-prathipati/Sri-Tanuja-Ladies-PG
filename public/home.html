About Us

Welcome to Sri Tanuja Ladies PG, located in the vibrant neighborhood of KPHB, Hyderabad! Situated in one of the city's most bustling areas, our hostel offers a convenient and accessible location for working people, students and guests.

With a total of five hostels, including four exclusively for girls and one hostel for boys, we cater to the diverse accommodation needs of our guests. Each hostel is thoughtfully designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and inclusive environment for residents.

At our hostels, you'll find modern amenities, spacious rooms, clean facilities, and a friendly atmosphere that fosters community and camaraderie among residents. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or seeking short-term accommodation, our hostels are the perfect choice for your stay in Hyderabad.
